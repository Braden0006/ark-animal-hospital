Surgery

At Ark Animal Hospital, we provide expert surgical care to keep your pet healthy and safe. Our experienced veterinarian uses modern techniques and monitoring equipment to ensure the highest standards of safety and comfort during every procedure. From routine spays and neuters to more complex surgeries, we provide compassionate care and attention for your pet. We’re here to support your pet’s recovery every step of the way.

Our common feline/canine procedures:

  • Spay/Neuter
  • Feline declaw
  • Preventative gastropexy
  • Cystotomy
  • GI/Foreign body surgery
  • Soft Tissue Surgery
  • Abdominal Surgeries
  • Extracapsular repair for torn cruciate stabilization

At Ark Animal Hospital, to ensure the best outcome for your pets surgical procedure, we do pre-surgical exams, pre-anesthetic bloodwork, IV fluid support, monitoring of vital signs, patient warming system, and post operative monitoring and recovery.
