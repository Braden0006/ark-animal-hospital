Veterinary
Hospital in Wyoming

For over thirty years Ark Animal Hospital, under Dr. James Thies, has provided quality medicine and compassionate care for our community. Now Dr. Scott Thorne, obtaining ownership in 2022, brings over twenty years of experience to the hospital. Dr. Thorne and his staff continue to provide a high level of compassionate care for our community.

We offer a wide range of services for canine and feline patients including routine vaccines, surgeries, dentals, critical care, and emergency services.

Our Services

Wellness Checkups

Your pets health is our highest priority. A routine and thorough exam is the first step in evaluating your pets overall health and detection of health concerns at all life stages of your pet.

Illness and Urgent Care

From time to time our pets become ill or injured and require medical care. These conditions are typically non-life threatening but should be evaluated by a veterinarian in a timely manner.

Emergency Services

Emergency veterinary care is immediate medical treatment for pets that have serious or life-threatening conditions.

Surgery

Experienced, compassionate surgical care for your pet’s health and safety

Pet Dentistry

As our beloved pets age, dental disease can become a developing and chronic health concern. We recommend a yearly dental evaluation to keep your pet happy and healthy.

Pet Boarding

When you are not at home for an extended period of time and need lodging for your pet, we can provide boarding services

About us

At Ark Animal Hospital we work to provide compassionate, high quality veterinary medicine for our community.  We recognize that animals are a gift from the Creator, and He has designated us to care for the animals.  Understanding this role, we appreciate the importance of animals in our lives, we will strive to develop a partnership with the family, the pet and the veterinary staff to honor our role in providing veterinary care. 

Dr. Thorne

Veterinary Doctor

Dr. Scott Thorne is a Wyoming native, growing up in Sheridan, Wyoming, graduating high school in 1989.  He earned an Associate’s Degree in Welding from Sheridan College and worked various welding jobs for several years.  Dr. Thorne went back to school earning a Bachelor of Science Degree in Zoology & Physiology at the University of Wyoming, graduating in 1997.  He and his wife, Christine, then moved to Grand Forks, North Dakota where Christine earned a Master’s Degree in Physical Therapy.  During those three years, Dr. Thorne worked as a welder and enrolled at the University of North Dakota completing additional coursework necessary to apply to veterinary medicine school.  Dr. Thorne was accepted to Colorado State University in 2000, earning his Doctor of Veterinary Medicine from the College of Veterinary Medicine and Biomedical Sciences at Colorado State University in May 2004.

Christine Thorne

Business Manager and Co-Owner

Christine’s love of animals was fostered from a young age, growing up on a ranch in Wyoming she “raised” multiple batches of kittens and stepped in for their mamas with around the clock bottle feedings when needed. When the opportunity came to purchase Ark Animal Hospital with her husband to help him realize his life-long goal of owning and running a veterinary hospital, she jumped at the chance to serve the community of Casper and its beloved pets. Christine is growing in her role of business manager as she is a physical therapist by educational training but enjoys the day-to-day challenges of business management. Christine loves spending time with her family, watching her girls grow up and become who God created them to be is the biggest blessing of her life. Christine also loves spending time with her cats Mitty, Jack, and Hobbes who bring a continual source of joy and entertainment to the lives of the Thorne Family.

Andrew

Andrew has been a Casper native his whole life and graduated from KW and Casper College. He went on to study Pre-vet at the University of Wyoming for 2 years. Andrew has been working in the veterinary field since he was 18 years old and his love and passion for the field has only grown. Starting his career at West Side Animal Hospital under Dr. Justin Johnson, moving to other local clinics and spending time in Laramie and Cody Wyoming. Andrew developed his skills and passion and later made the decision to attend Penn Foster for his Animal Science degree. Andrew has continued to further his education and clinical skills and now brings his culmination experiences to Ark Animal Hospital where he aims to bring a higher level of care; training new veterinary staff, and exploring new methods for veterinary care. His love for veterinary medicine stems from his desire to help animals in need and seeing the joy on families’ faces when their furry loved ones are brought back to them happier and heathier than before. Andrew is happily married to his wife Abby and in 2023 their family grew as they introduced their son Rory Wilder to the world. Andrew has had many furry family members over the years, both his own and working with foster organizations to help shelter dogs find fur-ever homes.
