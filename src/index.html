For over thirty years Ark Animal Hospital, under Dr. James Thies, has provided quality medicine and compassionate care for our community. Now Dr. Scott Thorne, obtaining ownership in 2022, brings over twenty years of experience to the hospital. Dr. Thorne and his staff continue to provide a high level of compassionate care for our community.

We offer a wide range of services for canine and feline patients including routine vaccines, surgeries, dentals, critical care, and emergency services.

About us

At Ark Animal Hospital we work to provide compassionate, high quality veterinary medicine for our community.  We recognize that animals are a gift from the Creator, and He has designated us to care for the animals.  Understanding this role, we appreciate the importance of animals in our lives, we will strive to develop a partnership with the family, the pet and the veterinary staff to honor our role in providing veterinary care.

Our Services

Wellness Checkups

Your pets health is our highest priority. A routine and thorough exam is the first step in evaluating your pets overall health and detection of health concerns at all life stages of your pet.

Illness and Urgent Care

From time to time our pets become ill or injured and require medical care. These conditions are typically non-life threatening but should be evaluated by a veterinarian in a timely manner.

Emergency Services

Emergency veterinary care is immediate medical treatment for pets that have serious or life-threatening conditions.

Surgery

Experienced, compassionate surgical care for your pet’s health and safety

Pet Dentistry

As our beloved pets age, dental disease can become a developing and chronic health concern. We recommend a yearly dental evaluation to keep your pet happy and healthy.

Pet Boarding

When you are not at home for an extended period of time and need lodging for your pet, we can provide boarding services
